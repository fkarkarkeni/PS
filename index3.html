What is e-learning?

E-learning, also called distance learning, is a service offering you the possibility of following an online training with the educational quality of face-to-face. Each e-learner chooses the course which corresponds to his desires and his motivation.

Who is concerned by the E-learning training?

Distance education concerns all people wishing to follow a diploma or qualifying training from home, anywhere in the world (students, adults, job seekers or schoolchildren). Whether you are in a professional activity, on leave or unemployed, the E-learning rhythm allows you to combine personal and professional life with your training.
